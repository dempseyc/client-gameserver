TTT - HIT - POW

Win by placing your mark 5 in a row, Vertical, Horizontal, or Diagonal through the center of the 5x5 grid.

There are 3 types of 'tiles' :

• 'TTT' X or O, can take an unoccupied square

• 'HIT' X or O, can steal an occupied square

• 'POW' X or  O, can take an unoccupied square, appears as a normal tile to your opponent. As  your next turn, all adjacent tiles are removed and it is your opponent's turn again.  Removed tiles are returned to their owner's 'deck'.

You start with a deck of tiles, shuffled, with 'Swap' being rare and 'Bomb' being more rare.  You hold three tiles in your hand, choose which one to play, and you are dealt another tile each time you place one on the board.  Tiles returned by 'Swap' or 'Bomb' are returned to the bottom of the owner's deck.

Click the tile from your hand that you want to place.  Then Click the square you want to play it in.

(optional) on 'X's first move, they can choose a starter board, which plays the first several opening rounds automatically in a typical fashion.
